# Resume Bullet Points

## Rental Property Management & Analytics System

Copy-paste ready bullet points for Data Analyst / Data Scientist resumes.

---

### Project-Level Bullet (1-liner)

- **Built an end-to-end rental property analytics system** using Python, SQL, and Streamlit, modeling 9 residential units, 1 commercial storefront, and 5 parking slots with 242+ records across 8 normalized tables, delivering occupancy tracking, revenue analysis, and cash flow visibility through an interactive dashboard.

---

### Technical Bullets (pick 3–5)

- Designed and implemented an **8-table normalized relational schema** in SQLite to model units, tenants, leases, payments, parking, storefront, and expenses with proper foreign keys and constraints.

- Wrote **20+ analytical SQL queries** using CTEs, window functions, conditional aggregations, and multi-table joins to compute KPIs including occupancy rate, net operating income, and payment delinquency metrics.

- Developed a **Python ETL pipeline** that loads CSV seed data into SQLite with dependency-aware ordering, producing a reproducible database build from a single command.

- Built a **25-check automated data validation framework** covering referential integrity, null detection, range validation, duplicate detection, and business rule enforcement.

- Created an **interactive Streamlit dashboard** with 8 KPI cards, 4 Plotly visualizations (cash flow trends, expense breakdowns, rent analysis, payment status), and floor-level filtering for property owners.

- Computed **10+ business KPIs** including occupancy rate (100%), on-time payment rate (96.2%), net operating income (NT$997K), and average rent per ping — supporting data-driven rental pricing and tenant screening decisions.
